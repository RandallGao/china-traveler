## What this helps you do

Land with a **short, realistic prep list** so you are not stuck at the airport juggling **maps, ride-hailing, payment SMS, and messengers** all at once.

**This edition assumes your first city is Shanghai.** Many people need **data or a clear connectivity plan** before the rest of the trip feels manageable. Skim this page once before you fly; go deeper in [Payments](../payments/), [Transport](../transport/), [Rail](../rail/), and the short [Connectivity](../connectivity/) chapter when you are ready.

## Step by step

Use this as a **pre-trip checklist** — top to bottom, a few days before departure:

- [ ] **Passport and entry rules** — Check validity (many countries want six months’ buffer) and any visa or entry steps your airline mentions. This guide is **not** legal advice; confirm with **your government’s foreign ministry** and the **Chinese embassy or consulate** that handles your jurisdiction.
- [ ] **Pick one wallet to finish first** — Decide whether **Alipay** or **WeChat Pay** will be your *first* app to complete end-to-end (see [Payments](../payments/)). Do not try to perfect both in the first hour on the ground.
- [ ] **Connectivity decision** — If you need **live maps, ride-hail, payment SMS, or chat apps** away from hotel Wi‑Fi, sketch a plan (roaming, eSIM, local SIM after arrival, or “Wi‑Fi only”). Details stay high-level in [Connectivity](../connectivity/).
- [ ] **Maps & addresses (高德 Amap)** — Install **[高德地图 (Amap)](https://www.amap.com/)** on your phone ([download](https://www.amap.com/download/)) and download an **offline pack for Shanghai** before you roam without data. For searching addresses and POIs in China, **Amap is the practical default** (Google Maps is often incomplete or blocked).
- [ ] **Offline safety net** — Save a **screenshot or note of your hotel name and address in Chinese characters** and star it in **Amap**.
- [ ] **Which Shanghai airport?** — Confirm **Pudong (PVG)** vs **Hongqiao (SHA)** on your ticket. They are far apart; your **airport-to-city** time and cost differ. Official hub: [Shanghai Airport Group — PVG & Hongqiao](https://www.shairport.com/) (English section: [shairport.com/ensh](https://www.shairport.com/ensh/index.html)).
- [ ] **Airport-to-city leg** — Know whether you will use **maglev + metro**, **metro only**, **taxi/ride-hail**, or a **pre-booked car** for *your* airport. Have **some cash** as a backup even if you plan to go cashless later.
- [ ] **Rail account (if you will take HSR soon)** — If you plan to buy **high-speed rail** tickets online, start account or app setup from home if the flow allows ([Rail](../rail/)); verification steps change over time.

## If this fails

If something is blocked before you leave (**app store region, bank SMS abroad, missing verification**), pack **cash**, **printed or saved Chinese address**, and plan to finish setup on **hotel Wi‑Fi** before you depend on apps for long trips.
